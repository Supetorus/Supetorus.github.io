Photon Blade Arena is a game made in just a couple of weeks for a class assignment at Neumont College of Computer Science. The player uses light blades to block incoming spheres which come from random directions. At first they come one at a time but over time they come faster and faster until it becomes overwhelming.

One of the difficulties of VR is moving the player throughout the space because when it is done wrong it can cause motion sickness. I addressed this issue by designing a game where the player never has to move from their starting position.
